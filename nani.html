NATURAL STAR NANI





**Nani**, whose full name is **Ghanta Naveen Babu**, is a popular Indian actor and producer primarily known for his work in Telugu cinema. He was born on **February 24, 1984**, in Hyderabad, India. Nani is often referred to as the "Natural Star" because of his versatile acting skills and ability to effortlessly portray a wide range of characters in a natural and realistic manner.

Career:

Nani started his career in the Telugu film industry in the mid-2000s, initially working as an assistant director before making his acting debut in the film **"Ashta Chamma" (2010)**. His performance in this comedy-drama film earned him recognition, and he quickly gained popularity for his charismatic screen presence and relatable performances. He continued to build his reputation with hits like **"Bhale Bhale Magadivoy" (2015)**, **"Nenu Local" (2017)**, and **"Jersey" (2019)**, which showcased his versatility as an actor. His role in **"Jersey"**, a sports drama about a former cricketer's journey to reclaim his passion, was particularly praised and won him several accolades.

Popular Films:

**"Eega" (2012)** – A fantasy film directed by S. S. Rajamouli, where Nani played a key role in a unique story about reincarnation.

**"Bhale Bhale Magadivoy" (2015)** – A romantic comedy where his portrayal of a forgetful protagonist was widely appreciated.

**"Nenu Local" (2017)** – A youthful, energetic performance that cemented his mass appeal.

**"Jersey" (2019)** – One of his career-defining roles as a passionate cricketer, earning critical acclaim.

Awards:

Nani has received several awards for his acting, including **Nandi Awards** and **Filmfare Awards**. His performances often resonate with audiences due to their authenticity and relatability, and he's known for his grounded, down-to-earth persona both on and off-screen.

Personal Life:

Nani is married to **Anjana Yelavarthi**, and they have a son named **Arjun**. Despite his fame, Nani maintains a relatively private life and is known for his humble and approachable personality. Nani's career continues to flourish, and he remains one of the most respected and beloved actors in Telugu cinema.
